How Rakshak Protects

Real-Time Risk Prediction

ML model analyzes 17 parameters including location, time, historical incidents and signal density to predict risk in real time — served via AWS SageMaker and Lambda with sub-200ms latency.

Instant SOS Dispatch

One tap triggers emergency contact alerts, patrol dispatch notification, and live location sharing simultaneously. Night Watch mode activates automatically when risk is high after 10 PM.

Judge Mode Simulation

Evaluators can simulate any time of day and Chennai pincode to see how the risk engine responds — live backend, real predictions, full transparency into the ML decision pipeline.

Infrastructure

Built on AWS

Every component is production-grade, serverless, and designed to scale across all of Chennai's 44 monitored zones.
